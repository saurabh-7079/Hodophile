About me

The idea about this blog came up when my friends were packing for a trip to Delhi. They haven’t gone anywhere in a while and were totally struggling with preparation and packing. Enter the Travel Addicted



I have had the opportunity to travel and had some experience with travel prep. With my help they were able to organize themselves and had a seamless trip. Later on I was thinking that there might be other people who might be interested in my tips and tricks for traveling.


My hope is to be able to help other travelers, especially beginner who are just starting to experience the life changing freedom of traveling and to inspire them to get out of their comfort zone and see our beautiful world.
